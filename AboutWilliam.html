About William Tell

We all know William Tell's famous overture, but do we know who William is?

A Famous Swiss Folk Figure

William Tell is one of the most famous people in Swiss folk lore. An expert marksman skilled with the crossbow, William assassinated Albrecht Gessler, a tyrannical leader. Although it was set in the early 14th century, this story was used as an important inspiration in many revolutions in Swiss history.
